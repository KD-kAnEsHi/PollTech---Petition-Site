The PolTech Manifesto

Our mission is to providing an easily accessible and unbiased platform where individuals can inform themselves about local candidates, issues, and the importance of various positions. This website aims to empower voters with the knowledge needed to make informed decisions, ultimately fostering increased civic engagement and representation.

Young Americans, have consistently shown lower voter turnout compared to older age groups, especailly when it comes to local elections despite the fact that local election arguiably impacts us and our society more than the federal elections. This sharp gap is due to the fact that young voters often face obstacles such as complex voter registration rules, lack of information on where and how to vote, and challenges in navigating the voting process.

This is where PolTech comes in. We are a group of young voters who are dedicate to inspire young Americans and low-income individuals to actively participate in shaping their future through the democratic process. Together, we aim to break down the barriers that hinder voter engagement, promoting a more inclusive and representative democracy for all. Join us in making a difference, one vote at a time.

Low Turn out in Local elections: The data shows that certain state laws and institutional practices, such as not mailing polling locations to voters, requiring early voter registration, and holding local elections separately from state/national cycles, are associated with lower voter turnout and greater incumbency advantage






Watch Some Videos

The Noise YouTube channel titled "Why Don’t Young People Vote? | Above The Noise" dives into the reasons behind low voter turnout among young people, particularly in local elections.
The video explores various factors that contribute to this trend, such as lack of information, feelings of disempowerment, and a general sense of disengagement from the political process. The series aims to cut through the hype and provide well-researched insights to help viewers understand the complexities of current issues, encouraging media literacy and civic engagement among teens and young adults​



The video from Nonprofit VOTE is about the importance of the 2020 Census and its implications for voting and representation, particularly for communities of color.
The video highlights how census data is crucial for redistricting, which determines how legislative boundaries are drawn and affects political representation. It emphasizes that an accurate census count ensures fair representation and equitable allocation of federal funds to local and state jurisdictions.
